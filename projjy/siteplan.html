Forex Coaching Website Plan

Site Name

FX Mastery Hub

This name reflects the site's purpose of helping individuals master the forex market through coaching and education. It communicates professionalism, focus, and expertise in forex trading.

Optional domain: fxmasteryhub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to offer educational resources, coaching services, and tools for aspiring forex traders. It will serve as a hub for learning, mentorship, and connecting with a professional forex coach.

Scenarios

  • How do I join your forex coaching program?
  • Where can I access free forex trading resources?
  • What strategy do you recommend for new traders with small capital?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – Deep Blue (#0d6efd): used for headings, nav links, and call-to-action buttons
  • Accent Color – Green (#28a745): used for success messages, highlights, and key icons

Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display (serif) – gives a professional, elegant feel
  • Body Text: Roboto (sans-serif) – clean and easy to read
